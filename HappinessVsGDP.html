Gross National Happiness vs GDP per Capita



The Happy Planet Index (HPI) is a metric that consider sustainability. It relies on the assumption that most people want to live long and fulfilling lives, and the country which is doing the best is the one that allows its citizens to do so, while also avoiding infringing on the opportunity of future people and people in other countries to do the same.


There is an extremely mild positive correlation between these two elements, but nothing statistically significant enough to draw a causative conclusion.
