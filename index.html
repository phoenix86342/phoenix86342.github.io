Summary: Global Geodemographic Analysis Across Countries



The purpose of this project was to determine the best approach to maximizing human development by identifying where we should be investing our time and resources. Investigated comparisons included each country's average annual inflation versus its gross national happiness, GDP per capita volatility versus GDP per capita, GDP per capita volatility versus gross national happiness, and as you see here, each country's gross national happiness versus its GDP per capita.
